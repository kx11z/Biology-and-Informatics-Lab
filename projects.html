Communicating Progress to 2D Cultured Neurons on MEA Chips

Summary: This project explores ways to communicate a sense of progress and information about simulated environments to 2D cultured neurons on microelectrode array chips.

Goal: The project responds to limitations in current neuron-training research, especially the lack of concrete progress signals during larger objectives for neurons in deep neural training environments.
